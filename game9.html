About CupHead

Cuphead is a 2017 indie run-and-gun game by Studio MDHR. It follows Cuphead and his brother Mugman, who must repay a debt to the Devil by collecting the souls of runaway debtors. The game features challenging levels and boss fights for one or two players, with a flexible narrative. As players progress, they unlock new powers and abilities, though only a few can be equipped at once.
